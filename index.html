About Nxt Smstr

Founded as a class project at Philip Merrill College of Journalism, nxt smstr was created to simplify the schedule-making process. By providing students with an aggregate of all of the information they need in an easily accessible format that can be searched by keywords, course and professor names, compatibility with schedule availability and ratings, nxt smstr empowers students to know all they need about a course prior to registration. University of Maryland students Jacob Orgel, Ally Tobler and Meghan Thompson used their own experiences and conversations with fellow undergrads to craft this platform in a manner that truly prepares students to embark on their next semester.
