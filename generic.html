About Nxt Smstr

the problem:

We found in our research that University of Maryland students experience a variety of setbacks when making their classes for the upcoming semester. Some of which include the need to switch back and forth between various websites, lacking in-depth information about the courses and professors, as well as the confusion surrounding degree requirements.


our solution:

We've streamlined the scheduling-making process by aggregating course and professor information and reviews, as well as an in-house, personalized course-creating feature that eliminates the need to use multiple websites at once.
